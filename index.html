Why Cadence

One voice. Every track. Your machine.

A voice that never drifts

Train once from a short reading session. Every track you generate afterwards sings in the same voice - yours - with no variation between generations.

Any language

Prompt in one language, sing in another. Lyrics and style understanding are driven by a language model with support for more than fifty languages.

Local by default

Generation runs on your own GPU. No subscription, no per-song cost, no upload. Cloud providers are an option you can add, never a requirement.

How it works

Four steps between a thought and a track

01 - DESCRIBE

Type a prompt

A sentence in any language. Mood, story, genre, tempo - as much or as little as you want.

02 - WRITE

Lyrics and structure

A language model turns your prompt into lyrics and a structured style: genre, tempo, key, mood.

03 - COMPOSE

Music generation

ACE-Step generates the full instrumental and a draft vocal on your GPU, in seconds.

04 - SING

Your voice

The draft vocal is re-voiced through your trained profile, keeping pitch and rhythm intact.

Under the hood

Native shell. Local engine. Real files.

A Rust desktop shell talking to a local Python engine over localhost. Tracks are WAV files on your disk, not entries in someone else's database.

Tauri 2 Rust Next.js FastAPI ACE-Step 1.5 RVC · Applio SQLite Ollama / Claude

Your voice stays home

Voice recordings and the trained model live on your machine and nowhere else. Nothing is uploaded during training or generation unless you explicitly switch a provider to cloud.
